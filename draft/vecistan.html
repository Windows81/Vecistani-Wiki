Vecistan

The Vecistani Great Communal People's Republic (VGCPR, also known as Vecistan /vejistan/), is a wholly metaversal country with sovereign territory in the Rōblox, Vyond, and Animaker metaverses. Vecistan was established in February 2018 during the Great War of Çıkış as a military junta and operates as a totalitarian state. Vecistan's economy is centrally planned, with unfettered oversight by an incumbent Vecistanous. The current Vecistanous is VisualPlugin.

Etymology

The name 'Vecistan' is derived from one of VisualPlugin's architectural works that was under construction at the time of the nation's establishment: Otel Vegəşlər. The latter name contains an Azerbaijanisation of the word 'Vegas' in plural form, after Las Vegas. Hence, a literal translation of the showcase could be 'Hotel Vegases'.

The long name of the state is the "Vecistani Great Communal People's Republic" (VGCPR). This name is formed from a backronym for the Video Game Cartoon Police (abbreviated as the VGCP), a police group that was active in Vyond at the time of Vecistan's founding.

Economy

Vecistan was established in February 2018 during the Great War of Çıkış as a centrally-planned military junta. Vecistan first got involved in Earthly affairs in early 2021 with the production of a Covid-19 vaccine, expanding into other industries in 2022 amid the gasoline crisis at the onset of the Russia-Ukraine war.
