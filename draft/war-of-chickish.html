The Great War of Çıkış

The Great War of Çıkış was a conflict involving the now-moribund nation of Gate 7 and Tutu Runner forming an alliance against VisualPlugin, and since 2018, Vecistan, that lasted between 30th July 2017 and 9th November 2019. The entirety of the conflict was fought within the Vyond metaverse.

Belligerants

VisualPlugin

In mid-2017, VisualPlugin expressed a desire to conquer Gate 7 without any substantial outside backing or military power, so as to retain full control of any new territory he may find. VisualPlugin has had a history of political-warfare expeditions in the Vyond metaversebhY9R7z7UVg.

Gate 7

VisualPlugin performed several insurrectionstNUrAFp_Ruo on Gate 7 territory during the war. Fighting with Gate 7 ended in March 2018 when VisualPlugin temporarily withdrew from the Vyond metaverse. CLarramore narrowly escaped arrest.

VisualPlugin sent a formal declaration of war in July 2017 to then-representative CLarramore of Gate 7, officially commencing the War of Çıkış. Both CLarramore and VisualPlugin kept a direct line of contact throughout the entire duration of the war. VisualPlugin initially avoided directly engaging in battle with CLarramore out of respect for her being three months senior to him, though this concession was reversed some time in 2018.

The first documented meeting of CLarramore and VisualPlugin took place on 2017-07-29FP3f-Qs3pBE, where they surfed a flying aeroplane to an undisclosed field near Baku, Azerbaijan.

Tutu Runner

Contrary to popular Vecistani belief, VisualPlugin first met Tutu Runner on friendly terms. Both parties met initially to negotiate transferring VisualPlugin's soul to a new body whilst keeping the previous one intactI3IVm-bvxkg. They reached an agreement that sometime in the future, Tutu Runner would initiate a ransomware attack on VisualPlugin's computer, giving him a vector for which to control VisualPlugin's original body.

Despite this encounter being VisualPlugin's first time meeting Tutu Runner, the inverse is not true. In July 2019, VisualPlugin travelled back in time to 24th December 2000 and ensnared Tutu Runner into transforming into a gingerbread man, effectively lobotomising him. This spell was undone after almost 17 years when one of VisualPlugin's allies attempted to trap his soul in an echo-chamber0DSOpZvUsZQ. Though the retransformation had not been documented, the incident triggered a chain reaction which eventually caused him to get his original likeness back and develop hostility against VisualPlugin.

Significant Battles

The First Incursion

VisualPlugin hired a Vyondic mercenary to infiltrate Gate 7's government offices on 2017-07-30tNUrAFp_Ruo. The invasion was successful despite the strong security prescence. CLarramore was summoned to the agent, which requested he be ordained a high rank in Gate 7's ascentionbound system. CLarramore obliged, having not been aware of the agent's mission. Once the ordainment procedure was complete, VisualPlugin reconvened with the agent and had himself given the same title. After recognising that CLarramore was three months older than to him, VisualPlugin settled not to engage in direct combat with her, allowing Gate 7 to remain intact.

The Californian Incursion

In an attempt to regain control, VisualPlugin summoned Theresa May to assist him in his takeover of Gate 7, to which she agreedzI2zMGhPOsA. In keeping to the original battle tenet of self-reliance, VisualPlugin had not ended up brokering a deal with any of the British Armed Forces. VisualPlugin remotely organised a sham immigration referrendum in Gate 7 with May as the leading Tory candidate. The results were 67% in favour for the pro-Conservative approach and 33% against. The results were convincing to CLarramore, who ceded due to 'cold, hard evidence' of majority support. Despite the narrow scope originally proposed in the referrendum, VisualPlugin used that as leverage to seize power in Gate 7's council. After a brief inauguration ceremony, the PBS Logo launched a surprise offence to abduct CLarramore and VisualPlugin. With full command of Gate 7 operations, the PBS Kids logo appointed Caillou as a puppet leader and murdered Gate 7's entire population, leaving only one survivor. CLarramore and VisualPlugin were eventually able to regain their consciousness and get Caillou deported.

In March 2018, CLarramore published an exposé piece on the recent implementation of Vecistan's dictadorial practicesYRt9iUPqSNw. Upon hearing of the story's release, Vecistan set a ploy in motion to bait CLarramore into Vecistani territory, then have her detained indefinitely for treason. Some days later, Tutu Runner successfully placed under the mind-control of VisualPlugin through an extremely-high-IQ intermediary. Tutu Runner, under the influence, cloned two manifestations of PBS Kids Logo's subservients, Caillou and Boris AndersonSseHYEclZOQ. However, unexpectedly for VisualPlugin, Tutu Runner immediately left the scene of battle - citing a sudden remembering of VisualPlugin's ability to respawn.

The official end to the War of Çıkış was marked by Tutu Runner's withdrawal from conflict in late 2019 after VisualPlugin reportedly gave him a life grounding sentence (i.e., solitary confinement)ap3kw6GC-7I. To maintain the narrative that the war was decisively won, Vecistani armed forces are deliberately not investigating the whereabouts of Tutu Runner.
