Bloxys

The Bloxys are an annual awards event show produced by the Rōblox Events Team and re-prepared by Vecistan into a 15-minute-long special broadcast to affirm VisualPlugin's leadership. Having been broadcast each year since 2018, the Bloxys special would reair hourly after the final production version is completed.

The 5th Bloxy Awards (2018)

Unlike with subsequent special broadcasts, a full recording had not been released outside of Vecistan. The internal broadcast had an airtime of five minutes despite only 53 seconds having been posted on the internet1. An unfinished rendition of the same scene had previously been released2, but had since been unlisted.

The 6th Bloxy Awards (2019)

The 6th Bloxy Awards special was the first of its kind to have a full uncut recording released online3. Highlights from the broadcast included creative endeavours from the Vecistani Industry of Creative Works, such as Wordslaught 4 and Homes in Dover 5. This broadcast, like future events, was under fire outside Vecistan for its extensive use of overdubbing characters from the original event.

The 7th Bloxy Awards (2020)

The 2020 Bloxy special placed a heavy emphasis on GoAnimate Wrapper, as the Bloxys special was the first major mass-media project since Wrapper's release. This special broadcast was also the first to tie in with a live face-to-face speech from VisualPlugin. The Vecistani Industry of Creative Works was commissioned to design the venue, as the location was set for the Rōblox province. An estimated total of 240 guests attended the speech on 2020-03-01. Following the event, guests would be instructed to perform a series of animations on loop. The whole event would be recorded on video, with select clips being used for the television special. Other locations designed by the ministry, such as Septicentricity 6, were shown.

The 8th Bloxy Awards (2021)

The 8th Bloxy Awards special broadcast emphasises the Kubla Khan 7, which had been a working project for a year. Unlike with previous broadcasts, parts of the 2021 special were shot in Earthly locations8 A couple of scenes were shot at the Las Vegas Strip.

The Innovation Awards

As the Bloxy specials are set to line up with their original counterparts, the 9th Bloxy Awards special were postponed to autumn 20229.
