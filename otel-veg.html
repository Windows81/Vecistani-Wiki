Developer

Engine

Rōblox

Otel Vegəşlər was the first official experience of the Rōblox province of Vecistan, which Vecistan itself was named after. Developed was commenced on 2017-11-20 solely by VisualPlugin then was placed under the Vecistani Industry of Creative Works' authorship following Constitutionsday. As of 2022-03-31, Otel had attracted over 10½×124 total visits since its creation.

History

The first stage of development (i.e., hotel rooms) was finished over 4 months, with the casino area having been slated to finish in late 2018. However, updates were ceased on 2018-07-23, owing to performance complications.

Reception

Initial opinion for Otel Vegəşlər was generally positive for the first few months of its existense. Soon before development was discontinued, Otel began to develop a negative reputation for lack of optimisation and game-breaking bugs upon entry. Despite enjoying a steady stream of visitors, as of 2022-03-31, the like-to-dislike ratio has been estimated to be 1:3 (i.e., one like for every three dislikes).
