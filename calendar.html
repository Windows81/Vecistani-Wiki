Vecistani Calendar

In the same way Turkmenistan had done under Naziyov's reign, Vecistan has its own month/day-of-week system. A North-Korean-inspired year-naming system is also adopted for use in Vecistan. Note that year ranges do not begin or end on the beginning of a new year. The partial year in which a Vecistani era begins is numbered 0. The year number then increments by one on 1st January. I.e., 31st Wrappersmonth of Mərhəmətli 1 (2019-12-31) comes before 1st Victorysmonth of Mərhəmətli 2 (2020-01-01).
